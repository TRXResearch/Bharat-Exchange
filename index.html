🛡️ SECURITY & TRANSPARENCY

How Bharat Exchange protects your funds

🔐
Cold storage majority
The large majority of user crypto is held in offline cold storage, with only operational balances kept in hot wallets.
📱
2FA on every account
Two-factor authentication is required for login, withdrawals, and changes to payout details.
🧪
Independent audits, published when complete
Third-party security reviews of our smart contracts and infrastructure will be linked here as soon as they are finalized.
🔑
Self-custody where it matters
On-chain swaps are signed from your own wallet — Bharat Exchange never has access to those private keys.
🏦
Segregated user funds
User balances are kept separate from operating funds.
🔍
Withdrawal address whitelisting
Optional allow-list to block withdrawals to unrecognized addresses.
📊
Real-time rate feeds
Market prices are pulled live rather than cached, reducing stale-quote risk.
Security is a practice, not a badge
